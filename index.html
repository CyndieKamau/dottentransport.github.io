Website Requirements

Domain, Hosting


The domain should have a maximum of 60 characters.
It should also portray exactly what the company does for SEO purposes.

Company Logo

Recommended as an affirmation that one is in the right website.

Tagline

Short phrase stating company's overall purpose.
Should be eye-catchy.

Reviews, Testimonials

Social proof. Very important.

  • How many clients you've worked with
  • No of staff
  • Years working as a transport company
  • No of successful deliveries

A customer feedback form will be present for any sort of feedback or inquiries.

Services

Services offered eg:

  • Containerized cargo
  • Perishables
  • Forklifts etc.

Contacts

All business contacts:

  • Email
  • Phone no
  • Social media platforms
  • Business location
  • Address

A contact form will be present.

Careers

This forum is for posting any vacancies, and all the qualifications needed.
A form for job enquiries will be present, with a section for uploading all necessary documents.

Add Ons

Gallery of work in progress, staff members, trucks etc.
Additionals such as applications for tracking trucks, any awards internal or external.
